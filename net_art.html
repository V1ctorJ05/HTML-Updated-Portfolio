The Cheese Revolt

My HTML Hyperlink Narrative Net Art is centered around cheese, a subject I chose because I like cheese as a food and is an ingredient in tons of my favorite foods. I also wanted to create something more playful and less serious compared to my other projects. I thought it would be fun to build a story involving the Springfield, Missouri Cheese Cave and the amount of different and unique cheeses that exist all over the world. When working with coding, I found it both exciting and rewarding when things came together. And watching the site being formed was fun. However, it was very frustrating when small errors kept the project from working correctly. And using GitHub was also challenging as it was extremely sensitive when trying to deploy and get the site to work. Through trial and error, I was able to shape the narrative into a humorous adventure where cheese begins to revolt and attempts to take over the world, leaving the only solution to lock it away in the cheese vault. Overall, this project reflects my interest in mixing creativity with technology while showing how even something simple, like cheese, can be the center of a larger and imaginative story. - Victor Jaramillo
